What constitutes a creative web project today?

I consider a creative web project one that takes advantage of the exciting features that the web has to offer to allow for engaging visual design and interesting methods of interactivity for its users.

How do you wish to employ the web or internet as a creative platform?

I want to use the web as a way of hosting my creative works of various mediums (video production, drawing, animating, etc) to create interactive projects that allow these works to build off of one another.

What are your goals in this course?

To learn fundamental programming skills that could be useful in a professional setting or for personal projects. Also, to make fun websites and share ideas with my classmates.
